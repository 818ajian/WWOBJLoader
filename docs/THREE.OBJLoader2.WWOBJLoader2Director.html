.OBJLoader2. WWOBJLoader2Director

new WWOBJLoader2Director()

Orchestrate loading of multiple OBJ files/data from an instruction queue with a configurable amount of workers (1-16). Use: prepareWorkers enqueueForRun processQueue deregister
